The ABX Solution

A decentralized marketplace with CAD-pegged pricing and smart contract settlement in ABX.

  • Merchants list goods in CAD; buyers pay in ABX.
  • Oracles convert CAD→ABX in real-time.
  • Staking & rewards drive long-term participation.

Flow (CAD-Pegged Pricing)

  1. Price set in CAD by merchant
  2. Oracle fetches CAD/ABX rate
  3. Smart contract collects ABX
  4. Merchant holds or converts
